Woman of Influence with Opinions - Ascend Android App

  • Google Play Listing

This project was the brainchild of Keith Hawke owner of Hawke Films based in Nelson, New Zealand. His daughters were approach the time to move into tertiary study or employment and had expressed a desire to hear from women who had been their and could give advice or anecdotes of being a women in the workforce, hence the Women of Influence project was born.
Later he approached WelTec to submit his project as part of the degree project paper.

I had the distinct privilege of forming a team with Pelise Tagata and Nick Rowley to undertake this project.

The purpose of this paper is to work through a commercial level project and demonstrate skills learned during our degree.

One of the interesting features of my team of three was that none of us knew each other or had previously worked together, but we still managed to gain an A+ grade. We were able to achieve a lot when working together as we could bounce ideas of each other and see how our different perspectives fitted in.

One of the challenges we faced was integrating a range APIs into our app specifically for Dropbox, Facebook, Google Firebase and Google+. To solve this we did a lot of research around this and many other aspect of the Android environment. Throughout the project all three of us were directly involved in all aspects of development, from analysis, design development testing dealing with the client and doing the administration, therefore I have a good understanding of how a project works at all levels.
