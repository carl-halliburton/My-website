Accessibility

I was born with a congenital eye condition called Aniridia, which can manifest itself in different ways and cannot lead to other conditions and problems. However the one thing common is that yo do not have any irises. I would like to note that my vision is considerable better than the norm for this condition.
This is one of the reasons I am in the technology and IT field, since I have used technology and IT tools to assist me throughout education.
I have a great interest in accessibility and how technology can be used to make people with any disability but particular blind and vision impaired people’s life’s better.
I will discuss this issue more from time to time in my blog linked to on the left side bar
